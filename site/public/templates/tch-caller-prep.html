How This Works
01
It's 45 minutes. Sharp.
We keep it tight because constraints create clarity. The clock runs the whole time — we'll keep us on track.
02
We lead. You react.
We'll share what we found in our research, name what we see, and pressure-test it with you. This isn't a brainstorm — it's a diagnosis + direction.
03
Off-topic ideas go to the B-Side.
If something great comes up that isn't on-agenda, we capture it separately. Nothing gets lost — it just gets filed for later.
04
Everything is recorded.
Don't worry about notes. The full session is transcribed and you'll get a link to review. Focus on the conversation, not capturing it.
05
Your action plan arrives after.
Within 48 hours, you'll receive a custom action plan with clear next moves, timelines, tools, and two paths for every recommendation — DIY or level up.

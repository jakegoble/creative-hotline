North Star Prompt — Select for Session
Opening Question — Pick the One That Fits This opens the session. Choose based on archetype + intake.
"If you could only sell one thing for the next year — what would it be and why?"
Best for: Too Many Ideas archetype. Forces the cut immediately.
"When someone asks what you do, what do you say — and does it feel true?"
Best for: Positioning confusion. Gets at the gap between identity and communication.
"What's the version of this brand that scares you a little?"
Best for: Playing it safe. Unlocks the bold direction they're sitting on.
"What would your brand look like if you had unlimited resources and zero fear?"
Best for: Constrained thinkers. Separates the vision from the limitations.
Both Or write a custom prompt for this client

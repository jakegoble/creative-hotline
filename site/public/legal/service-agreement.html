Service Agreement

This is a working draft. Final legal copy is pending review. By booking a Creative Hotline Call you agree to the terms below; any updates will be communicated via email and posted here. If anything's unclear, reply to your confirmation email and we'll talk.

1 · What you're booking

A Creative Hotline Call is a one-time, 45-minute, screen-shared strategy session with The Creative Hotline. You'll work through your most pressing creative challenges with us and walk away with a written Action Plan delivered within 24 hours of the call.

2 · What's included

  • One 45-minute video call (Google Meet)
  • An intake form completed before the call so we walk in dialed in
  • A written Action Plan delivered within 24 hours, hosted at a private link
  • A referral discount code you can share — you and your referral each save

3 · What it costs

The First Call is $499 USD (or whatever rate was published at the time of your booking). Payment is processed via Stripe at the time you book. We accept all major cards.

4 · Refunds + rescheduling

Rescheduling is free up to 24 hours before your call — use the link in your Calendly confirmation email. Cancellations within 24 hours of the call are non-refundable. If we cancel or reschedule on you, you'll get a full refund or a free reschedule, your choice.

5 · Recordings + Confidentiality

Calls are recorded for our internal note-taking and to inform your Action Plan. Recordings are stored privately and never shared outside The Creative Hotline team. We treat everything you share as confidential. We will not publish, repurpose, or reference your specifics without your written consent.

6 · What the Action Plan is (and isn't)

The Action Plan is professional creative strategy from our team — it is not legal, tax, medical, or financial advice. We'll point you at concrete next steps; you decide what to do with them. Outcomes depend on execution.

7 · Limits of liability

Our liability for any matter related to a Creative Hotline Call is limited to the amount you paid for the call. We are not liable for indirect, consequential, or speculative damages.
